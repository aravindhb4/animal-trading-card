White-Tiger

As a funfact, none have been seen in the wild for 50 years!

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Top Speed: 96kph (60mph)
  • Life Span: 10-20 years
  • Most Distinctive Feature: White fur with bright, blue eyes

The White Tiger (also known as the White Bengal Tiger) is a subspecies of Tiger, found throughout the Indian subcontinent.The White Tiger is a large and powerful animal that can weigh up to 300kg and reaches more than 3 meters in length.
